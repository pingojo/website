# Executive Summary

Pingojo is a revolutionary virtual recruitment platform that offers a unique service to both job seekers and companies. Our platform enables anyone to become a recruiter, matching job seekers with companies for a small commission fee. With our advanced contact management system and nicely designed dashboards, all parties involved can stay updated in real-time without compromising their privacy.

# The Opportunity

In today's job market, finding the right candidate for a job opening can be a challenging task for companies, while job seekers face the challenge of finding the right opportunity that matches their skills and experience. Pingojo addresses these challenges by offering a platform that connects companies with qualified candidates through third-party recruiters, based on the duration at each position in their resume.

# Market Analysis
The recruitment market is worth over $200 billion globally, and the demand for recruitment services continues to grow. There are several players in this market, but very few offer the unique value proposition that Pingojo provides. Our platform is designed to empower anyone to become a recruiter, giving companies access to a wider pool of candidates and job seekers access to more opportunities.
# Revenue Model

Our revenue model is based on a small commission fee that job seekers pay when they are matched with a job, a small processing fee that job seekers pay to enable more or less applications to be sent, and a small fee that companies pay when they hire a candidate. The recruiter who matches the job seeker with the job will also receive a percentage of the fees charged. Specifically:

1. Companies will pay 1/5 of the first paycheck or 500, whichever is less, when they hire a candidate.
2. Job seekers will pay 1/5 of the first paycheck or 500, whichever is less, when they are hired (deducted from their second payckeck).
3. Recruiters will get 30 cents for each job added to a seeker.
| Revenue Source | Fee |

# Marketing Strategy
Our marketing strategy will focus on building awareness of our platform through targeted advertising and partnerships with universities, training programs, and professional organizations. We will also leverage social media to reach a wider audience and build our brand.
# Financial Plan
Pingojo will start with a small budget and grow organically over the next three years. We will invest in developing our platform, building our team, and marketing our brand. Our revenue projections show that we will break even in year two and achieve profitability by year three.
# Technology
Our platform will make use of Web3 artificial intelligence technology, enabling us to show best matches and fill in the blanks where company contact information is not available. We will also offer a browser plug-in that overlays the statuses of job applications on Gmail, making it easy for job seekers to stay updated on their application status.
We will use AI ChatGPT to help with parsing job descriptions and resumes.

# System Workflow
1. A daily email is sent to companies with candidate match stats, enabling them to take action and be connected if they agree to the terms.
2. The system matches skills known about the company with the length of time there. It parses out all information in the resume and shows the company each position length and the time each skill was used at the job, preventing bias and providing an accurate assessment of the match.
3. When recruiters add jobs to the platform, they can see the job seekers' positions and skills, and best match them with the jobs. The $0.30 commission can be adjusted based on the match of keywords/skills in the job seeker's profile (pulled from the resume) and the job description pulled from the website.
